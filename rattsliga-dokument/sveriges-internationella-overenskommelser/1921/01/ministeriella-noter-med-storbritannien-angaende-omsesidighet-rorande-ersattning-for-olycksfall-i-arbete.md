# Ministeriella noter med Storbritannien angående ömsesidighet rörande ersättning för olycksfall i arbete

My Lord,
The first paragraph of Article 0 of the Swedish Law of July 5th, 1901, concerning compensa- tion for injuries received during work in consequence of accident ■contains certain discriminating provisions with regard to the right to compensation for such injuries in respect of foreigners. The Government, however, havé the right to grant exceptions to this regulation to citizens of a foreign country on conditions of reciprocity.
