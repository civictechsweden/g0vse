# Our Future with Asia - A Swedish Asia Strategy for 2000 and beyond

Framtid med Asien \- En svensk Asienstrategi inför 2000\-talet, på engelska

Our Future with Asia \- A Swedish Asia Strategy for 2000 and beyond

The report describes the Governments strategy for deepening and widening Swedens relations with Asia. The starting point is Swedens international interests and obligations as expressed in its foreign policy, and Swedens desire to promote its own welfare through economic growth and a high level of employment, coupled with the objectives and needs of the Asian countries. Date of publication: October 1999
