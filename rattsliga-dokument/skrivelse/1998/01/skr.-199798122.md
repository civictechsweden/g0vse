# Africa on the Move. Revitalising Swedish Policy towards Africa for the 21st Century

Afrika i förändring \- En förnyad svensk Afrikapolitik inför 2000\-talet, på engelska

Africa on the Move. Revitalising Swedish Policy towards Africa for the 21st Century

A comunication on Sweden's Africa policy submitted by the Swedish government to the Parliament. It reports on the political and economic situation in Africa as well as Sweden's contacts with the continent. Year of publication: 1998
