# Welfare in Sweden: The Balance Sheet for the 1990s

Engelsk version av "Välfärdsbokslut för 1990-talet (SOU 2001:79)" - slutbetänkande från Kommittén Välfärdsbokslutet
In this report, the Welfare Commission presents, in accordance with its instructions, a balance sheet for developments in welfare in Sweden in the 1990s. The introduction which follows here outlines the Commissions assignment and the material upon which the final report is based. It also briefly describes the main conceptual standpoints and definitions of welfare which guided the Commissions work and some of the restrictions which we chose to make. It concludes with an outline of the structure of the Report.
