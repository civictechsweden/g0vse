OECD strategy for Sweden \- Overall Priorities and Organisation

The purpose of this report is to establish an OECD strategy for Sweden. The report focuses on Sweden's lines of action in OECD and on the forms and methods of OECD work. The strategy is intended to provide guidance both for future Swedish action in OECD and for how Sweden should organise OECD work, mainly such work done in the Government Offices.
