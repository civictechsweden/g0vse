# Strategy for development cooperation with the Democratic Republic of Congo, 2009-2012

This strategy sets out the direction of Sweden´s development cooperation with the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC) for the period 2009-201. The strategy is based on Sweden´s policy for global development (PGD) with the objective of contributing to equitable and sustainable global development and on the objective of Swedish development cooperation, to help to create conditions for poor women and men to improve their living conditions.
