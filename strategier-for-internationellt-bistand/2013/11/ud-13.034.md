# Results strategy for Sweden’s international development cooperation in South Sudan 2014 – 2016

This results strategy governs the use of funds allocated under appropriation item 9 Africa in Sida’s appropriation directions for the relevant financial year. The strategy is to apply for the period 2014–2016. In all, the strategy comprises some SEK 525 million over the next three years.

The aim of Sweden’s development cooperation commitment in
South Sudan is to contribute to improved survival and health among women and children. Sweden’s support is also intended to promote respect for human rights and thereby stronger democracy in South Sudan.
