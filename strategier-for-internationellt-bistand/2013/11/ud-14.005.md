# Results strategy for Sweden’s international development cooperation in Sudan 2014 - 2016

This results strategy governs the use of funds allocated under appropriation item 9 Africa in Sida’s appropriation directions for the relevant financial year. The strategy is to apply for the period 2014–2016\. All in all, the strategy comprises some SEK 360 million over the next three years.

Sweden’s aid commitment in Sudan is aimed at reducing the risk of a return to conflict. Sweden’s support is also intended to promote respect for human rights and thereby strengthen democracy in Sudan.
