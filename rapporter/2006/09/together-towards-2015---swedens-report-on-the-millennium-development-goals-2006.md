# Together towards 2015 - Sweden's Report on the Millennium Development Goals 2006

"Together towards 2015\..." is Sweden's second report on the Millennium Development Goals (MDGs). It describes what has happened primarily with Goal 8 and 7 since the former report from 2004\. It is mainly the policy for global development, that has developed during the last two years.
Annex 1 describes how Sweden works with Goal 1\-6\.
