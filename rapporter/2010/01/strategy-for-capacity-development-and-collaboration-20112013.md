# Strategy for capacity development and collaboration 2011–2013

The strategy’s areas of activity are:
\- capacity and institutional development of organisations in developing countries through international training programmes (ITP),
\- resource base development (primarily through Swedish staff working in multilateral organisations and the EU, training programmes in Sida’s field operations, and scholarship, exchange and trainee programmes),
\- collaboration with and support through business sector actors (Business for Development/B4D), and
\- Information and Communication Technology for Development (ICT4D).
